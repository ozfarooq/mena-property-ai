The Problem

Compliance is broken
for regulated SMBs

Enterprise GRC tools cost $10K–$20K/month. Spreadsheets miss deadlines. One lapsed renewal = fines, suspended licences, or reputational damage.

📋

No affordable tooling

Enterprise platforms like OneTrust and Vanta start at $10K+/month. SMBs fall back on spreadsheets and calendar reminders that carry no process context.

🌐

Patchwork of regulations

A healthcare clinic in California with an AI scheduling tool faces HIPAA, state AI law, ONC certification, and JCAHO accreditation — all with different renewal cycles.

⚠️

High stakes for missing deadlines

EU AI Act penalties reach €35M or 7% of global turnover. HIPAA fines run up to $1.9M per violation category. Missing a SOC 2 renewal can kill enterprise deals.

The Product

Six screens that run
your compliance operation

From deadline tracking to document vault to one-click audit reports — everything in one place.

01 Dashboard

Compliance Health Overview

  • Compliance health score per agency (Red / Amber / Green)
  • Upcoming renewals in next 30 / 60 / 90 days
  • Overdue items banner with escalation prompt
  • Quick-add renewal button
02 Tracker

Compliance Tracker

  • Table: Compliance | Industry | Jurisdiction | Due date | Owner | Status
  • Filter by industry, status, urgency, assigned user
  • Calendar view toggle for timeline visibility
  • Days-remaining countdown per item
03 Renewal

Renewal Detail Page

  • Pre-loaded step-by-step renewal checklist per compliance type
  • Document vault — upload & version-control evidence files
  • Assignee + due date per checklist task
  • One-click PDF export for regulators
04 Agency Mgmt

Agency Management

  • All client agencies in one sidebar (Consultant tier)
  • Per-agency compliance health overview
  • White-label toggle per client
  • Client-facing report generation
05 Notifications

Notifications Centre

  • Alerts at 90 / 60 / 30 / 7 days before expiry
  • Escalation chain config — notify right person if no action
  • Email + WhatsApp/SMS channel settings
  • Event-triggered alerts (e.g. AI model update → HIPAA review)
06 Settings

Settings & Compliance DB

  • Industry selector: Healthcare / Fintech / HR / Custom
  • Jurisdiction: US state-level / UAE / KSA / EU
  • Auto-populated renewal catalogue on selection
  • Team member roles & permissions

Target Markets

US first. Middle East
as Phase 3 expansion.

Two markets with different entry dynamics — both underserved by existing compliance tooling.

🇺🇸

United States

HIPAA, SOC 2, NYC LL144, Colorado AI Act — a layered patchwork of obligations with no affordable unified tracker. SMBs in healthcare, fintech, and HR are the most exposed segment.

Ideal Customer Profile

Healthcare clinic operator or fintech CTO/COO, 10–100 staff, currently managing renewals on spreadsheets. Acutely aware of the risk. No compliance staff.

Go-to-Market

Self-serve freemium via ProductHunt launch. SEO content targeting "HIPAA renewal checklist" queries. LinkedIn outreach to healthcare ops and fintech COOs.

Competitive Gap

What every competitor
is missing

01

No affordable SMB tool

Everything below $500/month is a generic reminder app. No affordable, industry-specific renewal management product exists for regulated SMBs.

02

No GCC localisation

Zero compliance SaaS tools cover UAE PDPL, Saudi NDMO AI frameworks, or AAOIFI Islamic Finance standards natively. The entire GCC market is underserved.

03

Reminders without process

Existing tools alert you a deadline is coming. None tell you the 12 steps to complete it, who owns each, what documents are needed, or how to generate the evidence file.

04

No event-triggered logic

No tool detects that "you updated your AI model → this resets your HIPAA review clock." That event-to-obligation intelligence is a clear moat if built first.

05

No pre-loaded templates

Generic GRC platforms list 200 frameworks with no guidance. ComplianceSync ships with pre-loaded, step-by-step renewal checklists specific to each compliance type.

06

No consultant tier

Compliance consultants managing 5–15 clients use spreadsheets and email. A white-label multi-agency dashboard at $399/month is an obvious product for this segment.
